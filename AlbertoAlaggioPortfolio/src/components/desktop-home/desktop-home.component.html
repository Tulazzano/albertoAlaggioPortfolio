ABOUT

Always passionate about fashion and photography, after his studies in cultural anthropology at the University of Turin, Alberto – born 1993 – began traveling to Asia alone, backpacking with his camera in hand.
On his return he settled in Milan, where he began to publish documentary and fashion projects on various internationally renowned magazines. His works are characterized by a balanced dualism between reportage and fashion, analogue photography and digital photography, with an eye towards geometry and the static nature of the subjects he photographs.
Alberto always tries to keep his eye trained, traveling the world to discover places never seen before and seeking his inspiration from the journey itself.
